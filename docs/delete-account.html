Delete Account

Solar Merge  ·  Permanently remove your data

⚠ This action is permanent Deleting your account will permanently erase your leaderboard score, display name, and all associated data. This cannot be undone.

What Gets Deleted

  • Your Google account sign-in session in the app
  • Your leaderboard entry (score, drop count, display name)
  • Your Firebase Authentication account linked to Solar Merge

Your Google account itself is not deleted — only the data associated with Solar Merge.

How to Delete Your Account In-App

1
Open Solar Merge on your device.
2
Tap the Leaderboard button on the main screen.
3
If you are signed in, scroll to the bottom of the leaderboard panel and tap "Delete Account".
4
Confirm the deletion in the dialog that appears. Your data will be permanently deleted immediately.

Request Deletion by Email

If you cannot access the app or prefer to request deletion manually, send an email to:

iskaeldotcom@gmail.com
Subject: Delete Account – Solar Merge
Include the email address associated with your Google account.

We will process your request and confirm deletion within 7 business days.

Data Retention After Deletion

Once deleted, your data is removed from our Firestore database immediately. Firebase Authentication records are purged within 30 days in accordance with Google's data retention policies. No backups retain personally identifiable information beyond 30 days.
